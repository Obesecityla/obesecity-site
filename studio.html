ABOUT

OBESECITY is the creative identity of Paul Chavez, a Los Angeles-based multi-disciplinary artist working across photography, music production, publishing, and software development.

The work is connected — the same eye that frames a photograph on the street informs how a beat is structured, how a zine is sequenced, how an app is designed. Everything is the same creative impulse in a different form.

Not a brand. An artist. The distinction matters.

WORK

Photography: Street, documentary, and portrait work shot primarily on film. Published across three self-produced zine volumes — Los Angeles, New York, and Chicago — with prints available in limited editions.

Music: Instrumental beat production under the OBESECITY name. "On The Rocks" (2023) is the latest full-length release. Available for sync licensing in film, television, and advertising.

Publishing: Three-volume zine series and "For The Children," a self-published children's picture book.

Software: Independent mobile app development. Built from the ground up, released independently on iOS and Android.
